Recipe #1

  • Time: _10 mins
  • Serves: _EGG ROLL
Ingredients Quantity
egg 2
medium onion 1
medium capsicum(green pepper) 1
tomato ketchup two tablespoon
carrot 1 piece
salt as required
chapati 1
green chilli sauce 1/2 teaspoon
lemon juice 1/2 teaspoon
powered black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
butter 2 teaspoon
ginger 1 inch

Step 1:Cut thick slices of onion and capsicum, grate the carrots, mince the ginger and keep aside.
Take a frying pan and put it on medium heat.
When it is sufficiently hot add one teaspoon of butter and the onions, carrots and capsicum. Cook on high flame for 2 minutes.
Add salt, pepper, lemon juice, tomato ketchup, chilli sauce and cook for another one minute. Remove from fire and keep aside.

Step 2:In a bowl, break the eggs.
Add salt and pepper to taste and whisk well.
Heat one tsp butter in the pan.
Add half of the egg mixture and make an omelette.
Put the chapati over it.
Top it with the rest of the egg mixture and flip so that it is cooked on both sides.

Step 3:Spoon the cooked capsicum, carrot and onion mixture in the center of the chapati.
Top with some raw onions, roll and serve hot with tomato ketchup.

Recipe #2

  • Time: _10 mins
  • Serves: _CHINESE MAGGI
Ingredients Quantity
maggi noodles 1 pack
oil 1 tablespoon
finely chopped garlic cloves 2
ginger, julienned 1/2 inch
chopped spring onions(white part) 1 tablespoon
carrots, thinly sliced 1/4 cup
chopped spring onions(green part) 1/4 cup
capsicum, sliced 1/4 cup
soya sause 1 teaspoon
tomato ketchup 1 tablespoon

Step 1:Cook Maggi according to package instructions without the tastemaker. Drain and set aside.

Step 2:Heat oil in a pan and add ginger garlic. Saute on high flame for a minute and add spring onions, carrots and capsicum. Stir fry for a minute and add soy sauce and ketchup.

Step 3:Add the maggi noodles and toss well. Cook for a minute and switch off the flame. Serve immediately.

Recipe #3

  • Time: _30 mins
  • Serves: _VEG FRIED RICE
Ingredients Quantity
rice(long grain) 2 cups
garlic(chopped) 1 tablespoon
olive oil or sesame oil two tablespoon
soya sauce 1 tablespoon
vinegar(apple cinder or white vinegar) 3/4 to 1 tablespoon
salt as needed
pepper(coarsely crushed)
carrots(chopped finely) 1/4 cup
bell pepper(or capsicum chopped finely) 1/4 cup
beans(chopped finely) 1/4 cup
cabbage(or 3 to 4 tablespoon chopped) 1/4 cup
spring onions(or 3 to 4 tablespoon chopped) 1/4 cup
green chillislit 1
sprigs celerychopped finely 8
mushroomssliced 1/4 cup
star anise 1
strand mass 1

Step 1:Heat a wok with oil on the highest flame and fry chopped garlic slightly.

Step 2:Add mace and star anise(optional) for a wonderful flavour.

Step 3:Next put in all the chopped veggies and fry for a min or two.

Step 4:Add in half tsp sugar(optional) to cut down the pungent taste from vinegar.

Step 5:To make Indian fried rice recipe just add vinegar & salt to the wok.

Step 6:To make Chinese style, add soya sauce, vinegar and very little salt.

Step 7:Add rice and pepper powder. Fry the vegetable fried rice for 2 min.

Step 8:Garnish veg fried rice with flesh chopped spring onions.
